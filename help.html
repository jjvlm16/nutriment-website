FAQs

What is Nutriment?

Nutriment is a BMI (Body Mass Index) website calculator that computes the BMI of the user using metric system BMI = weight(kg) ÷ height(m)² with recommendations to improve, maintain and decrease the nutritional status of the user back to normal.

Why did you conduct this research?

We have selected this topic due to an observed discrepancy in today's youth where their weight and height often does not match to their age, resulting in issues such as being underweight, overweight, or obese due to malnutrition. As a result, we aim to conduct research to raise awareness among our peers about the importance of health maintenance as it is our most valuable asset.

How does your website work?

1 Measure your weight in kilograms (kg). 2 Measure your height in meters (m). 3 Square your height in meters (height x height). 4 Divide your weight in kilograms by the square of your height to calculate BMI (weight / (height x height)). Interpret the BMI value as follows: - Below 18.5: Underweight - 18.5 - 24.9: Normal weight - 25 - 29.9: Overweight - 30 and above: Obese
